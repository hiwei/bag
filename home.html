Choose Carefully.

　A properly-sized backpack will make the experience much more enjoyable. The weight of your pack is another important consideration. When selecting a pack, begin by choosing from the two basic designs: internal or external frame. In either design, you'll need to choose the right size pack. A 40-liter or smaller pack is suitable for a weekend hike. Something between 40 and 70 liters in capacity is a good size for a 1-2 week hike. I recommend you work with a knowledgeable sales person who can help you select the design, capacity and fit for your hike.
